About Us

International Association of Leaders in Higher Education

Who we are

AILES brings together a diverse community of leaders, including university presidents, academic administrators, researchers, and policymakers, from around the globe. Our members share a passion for education and a commitment to shaping the future of higher education.

What we do

Through conferences, workshops, and networking events, AILES provides opportunities for leaders in higher education to connect, share ideas, and learn from one another. We also collaborate on research projects, advocacy initiatives, and capacity-building programs to address the most pressing challenges facing higher education today.

Founded in 2024

Our values

  • At AILES, we uphold a commitment to excellence in all aspects of higher education. We strive to promote academic rigor, innovation, and continuous improvement to ensure the highest standards of quality and effectiveness in teaching, research, and service.

  • Integrity is at the core of everything we do at AILES. We believe in honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in our interactions with stakeholders and the broader community. By upholding the highest standards of integrity, we build trust and credibility in our organization and the field of higher education.

  • AILES is dedicated to fostering an inclusive and diverse community of leaders in higher education. We embrace diversity in all its forms and seek to create a welcoming environment where everyone feels valued, respected, and empowered to contribute their unique perspectives and experiences.

  • Collaboration is fundamental to the work of AILES. We believe in the power of partnership and cooperation to drive positive change and innovation in higher education. By working together across institutions, sectors, and borders, we can leverage collective expertise and resources to address complex challenges and achieve shared goals.
